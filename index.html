T2 Reservation Management System

Status: Completed

As part of a team of three Danil, Mat and I had completed an ASP.Net Core Web Application, T2 Reservation Management System (T2RMS). This application was designed to take reservation made by Management, Staff and Members, we had extended the application where Managers and Staff can place reservations on tables via the creation method of selecting tables or a scheduler with a drag and drop feature. Working in an agile environment, Azure DevOps allowed our team to track our progress using the SCRUM process, checking in daily with updates of progress on weekly tasks.

This application shows the complex use of RESTful API’s, JavaScript, C#, SQL and Asynchronous programming. We have also used various plugins to meet satisfaction of our team’s agreements in its features and functionalities. I have added the assessment criteria "Application_Development_AE_Project", if you like to see the answers to the assessment please contact me via email.

My teammates and I have the intention to keep working on the projects by refactoring and further enhancing the current features and also adding more when possible.
